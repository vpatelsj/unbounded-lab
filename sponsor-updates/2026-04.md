# 2026-04 \u2014 Wave 1 closer

Wave 1 is shipped end-to-end. Public Open WebUI, two engines (Ollama +
vLLM) on two GB10 Sparks, real TLS, real metrics, real reproducible
benchmarks. Numbers and pain measured rather than asserted.

## What shipped

- **W1.1** Ollama + Open WebUI on `spark-3d37`. Q4_K_M GGUF (18.6\u202fGB),
  serving from the public hostname.
- **W1.2** vLLM on `spark-2c24`. Qwen3-30B-A3B-GPTQ-Int4 (16\u202fGB),
  Ollama-compat shim sidecar in front. KV pool 9.31\u202fGiB / 101\u202f712 tokens
  at `--gpu-memory-utilization=0.22` (capped by ~65\u202fGiB pinned host
  page-cache, see W1.3).
- **W1.3** Storage pain journal opened. Three rows so far covering FP8
  abandon, GPTQ-Int4 land, and Xet leftover hygiene.
- **W1.4** ingress-nginx + cert-manager, real Let's Encrypt cert at
  `vapa-ollama.canadacentral.cloudapp.azure.com`.
- **W1.5** kube-prometheus-stack + dcgm-exporter pinned to GB10 nodes.
  Prometheus and Grafana on persistent disks, vLLM `/metrics` scraped.
- **W1.6** Stdlib-only Python bench harness (`bench/lab_bench.py`),
  reproducible via `make w1.6-run-vllm` / `make w1.6-results-fetch`.

## Headline numbers (vLLM, Qwen3-30B-A3B-GPTQ-Int4, spark-2c24)

| Concurrency | Aggregate decode (t/s) | p50 latency (s) | Per-req decode (t/s) | Peak GPU power (W) |
|------------:|-----------------------:|----------------:|---------------------:|-------------------:|
|           1 |                  62.5  |            2.05 |                 62.5 |               32.3 |
|           4 |                 199.6  |            2.21 |                 49.9 |               35.8 |
|           8 |                 299.2  |            2.47 |                 37.4 |               35.8 |
|          16 |                 462.2  |            2.81 |                 28.9 |               35.8 |

Linear-ish speedup to c=16. p50 only doubles for 7\u00d7 throughput. GB10
saturates at ~36\u202fW under load \u2014 small chip, big-batch friendly.

DCGM on Spark Tegra silently does not export `DCGM_FI_DEV_FB_USED`, so the
bench harness uses `DCGM_FI_DEV_POWER_USAGE` peak as the "GPU was busy"
signal. The harness will flip back to FB on GB200 in Wave 2.

## Storage pain measured

- 31\u202fGB FP8 weights downloaded then abandoned (vLLM 0.11.0 + GB10 sm_121a
  doesn't compile FP8 Marlin kernels yet) \u2014 row 1 of journal.
- 16\u202fGB GPTQ-Int4 landed; 9.4\u202fGB HF Xet cache leftover \u2014 row 2.
- ~65\u202fGiB unreclaimable host page-cache on `spark-2c24` after warm
  traffic, capping `--gpu-memory-utilization` at 0.22 \u2014 row 3.
  Reboot runbook drafted ([docs/runbooks/spark-reboot.md](../docs/runbooks/spark-reboot.md)).
